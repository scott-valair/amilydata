Questions

A clear starting point.

You do not need perfect data before reaching out. Messy data is normal.

What kind of data can you help with?

Spreadsheets, lists, donor records, sales records, attendance logs, survey results, and similar files.

Do I need a full project scope first?

No. A short conversation and a sample file are enough to decide what is useful and realistic.

Are volunteer or low-cost projects available?

Yes. Amily Data accepts select discounted or pro bono projects for community-focused organizations.
